The problem we're solving

Why this product matters for RoW customers

Today's pain points

  • Rely on fragmented local rails with no SEPA access
  • Funding friction across non-SEPA regions
  • No standardised global USD account experience
  • Limited cross-border banking capability

What this unlocks

  • Standardised cross-border USD funding via SWIFT MT103
  • Expansion into LATAM, GCC & APAC markets
  • Global USD account with attached Visa debit card
  • Foundation for future outbound SWIFT capability

Product definition

Three pillars of the USD SWIFT IBAN proposition

🏛️

Account

  • USD only — no FX, no SEPA
  • SWIFT rail exclusively
  • Standalone IBAN (no multi-currency)
  • Inbound MT103 (Phase 1 only)
  • No outbound SWIFT at launch
💳

Card

  • USD Visa Debit
  • Virtual-first issuance
  • Physical card (tier-based)
  • Direct ledger authorisation
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay supported
💸

Funding

  • Me-to-Me only (first-party)
  • No third-party inbound
  • No outbound SWIFT in Phase 1
  • Card spend debits USD ledger
  • 1–5 day SWIFT processing time
